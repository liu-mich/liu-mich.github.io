## About Me

I am an incoming Politics Ph.D. student and Centennial Fellow in the Social Sciences and Humanities at Princeton University. My research interests include public opinion, political communication, political psychology, and survey/experimental methods. 

I hold a BA from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill and wore many hats before graduate school: analyzing surveys at the Pew Research Center, working at a domestic violence protective agency, and conducting interviews on environmental public opinion in North Carolina communities. I am from Charlotte, NC.
